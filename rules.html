    • PokéBeans Rules

    In order to maintain a fun and inclusive environment for all members, we have established the following rules:

    1. Respect all members: Treat all members with kindness, respect, and inclusivity. Do not engage in any form of bullying, discrimination, or harassment.
    2. Follow Pokémon Company's policies: As a Pokémon society, we adhere to the policies and guidelines set forth by the Pokémon Company. This includes not engaging in any form of cheating, hacking, or using unauthorized third-party software.
    3. No spamming or inappropriate content: Do not engage in spamming or post any content that is inappropriate, offensive, or violates any laws, including but not limited to explicit language, explicit images, or illegal activities.
    4. Respect intellectual property: Do not use or share any copyrighted or trademarked material without proper permission. This includes but is not limited to Pokémon images, logos, or any other copyrighted content.
    5. Keep it family-friendly: This is a family-friendly community, so please keep all content, discussions, and interactions suitable for all ages. Avoid engaging in discussions about sensitive topics such as politics, religion, or explicit content.
    6. No personal information: Do not share any personal information of yourself or others, including but not limited to full names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.
    7. Respect moderators and their decisions: Our moderators are here to ensure that the rules are followed and to maintain a safe environment. Please respect their decisions and do not engage in arguments or disrespectful behavior towards them.
    8. Report any issues: If you encounter any issues, such as violations of the rules or inappropriate behavior, please report it to the moderators or administrators immediately.

    Failure to comply with these rules may result in warnings, temporary or permanent bans, or other actions at the discretion of the moderators or administrators.
